A bit about me:

Hi, I’m Yasuhiro Hayashi
Data Scientist, DB Researcher, Associate Professor

Yasuhiro Hayashi is an associate professor at Faculty of Data Science, Musashino University, Japan. He earned Ph.D. (Media and Governance) from Keio University in 2011. He specializes in database and its application in educational technology. His major research interests are the application of cyber-physical systems that integrate physical and cyber space, data mining, and semantic computing. He also joins collaborated research projects with many companies and communities.
